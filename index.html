From Evidence to Analysis in Four Steps

Our streamlined workflow takes you from raw evidence files to court-ready exhibits with minimal manual intervention.

1

Upload Evidence

Drag and drop BWC videos, police reports, CAD logs, photos, or any discovery documents. SHA-256 checksums are generated immediately to preserve chain of custody. Supports MP4, MOV, PDF, JPG, PNG, CSV, JSON, DOCX, and more.

2

AI Processing

Our local AI tools run automatically: Whisper transcribes audio, pyannote identifies speakers, Tesseract extracts text from documents, spaCy finds names and dates, YOLOv8 detects objects. All processing happens on your machine—nothing sent to cloud servers.

3

Search & Analyze

Use semantic search to find relevant passages across thousands of pages. Query by natural language ("when did Officer Smith arrive?") instead of exact keywords. Build timelines, cross-reference evidence, identify contradictions automatically.

4

Export Court-Ready Exhibits

Generate professional evidence packages with transcripts, enhanced media, annotated timelines, and chain of custody documentation. Export to PDF, Word, or structured JSON. All exports include verification checksums.
