# Barber Cam — FAQ

## What is Barber Cam?

Barber Cam is a citizen-led accountability initiative focused on ethical documentation, due process, and dignity. The goal is to preserve accurate records and reduce harm through responsible recording practices.

## Is Barber Cam law enforcement?

No. Barber Cam is not a government agency, not law enforcement, and not affiliated with any department.

## Is this “surveillance”?

No. Barber Cam is not designed for mass monitoring or covert tracking. It is intended for transparent, lawful, and ethical documentation in specific contexts where recording protects the truth and all parties involved.

## Does Barber Cam encourage confrontation?

No. The guiding principle is restraint: record to prevent escalation, not to provoke it. We emphasize de-escalation, clarity, and safety.

## How will recordings be handled?

Barber Cam’s approach is stewardship-first: minimal collection, careful handling, and ethical use standards. Details will be published only after governance and safeguards are finalized.

## Is this legal?

Recording laws vary by location and situation. Barber Cam does not provide legal advice. Users are responsible for complying with applicable laws, consent rules, and venue policies.

## Is this anti-government?

No. Barber Cam is pro-accountability and pro-due-process. The purpose is to preserve truth, reduce harm, and support fair outcomes.

## What stage is the project in?

Active development and validation. This repository is a public preview only; it does not contain operational software or internal systems.

## Can I contribute?

Not yet for core systems. When contribution pathways open, they will be governed by clear rules, licensing, and review processes.

## How can I stay informed?

Use the contact method on the landing page or open an issue in this repository with “Updates” in the title.
