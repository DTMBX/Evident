# Government & Municipal Partnerships

Faith Frontier Ecclesiastical Trust is ready to partner with New Jersey municipalities, counties, and state agencies to address **homelessness, reentry, land stewardship, and community restoration**.

---

## Our Value Proposition

### What Makes Faith Frontier Different

**Transparency & Accountability:**
- All court filings and case records publicly documented at [faithfrontier.org/cases](/cases/)
- Financial records and governance structure openly published
- Quarterly reporting on outcomes and spending
- Independent audit trail through legal proceedings

**Proven Competence:**
- Successfully navigating complex litigation in state and federal courts
- Constitutional literacy and procedural expertise
- Experience with ADA compliance, fee waivers, discovery, and appellate process
- Track record available for review

**Cost-Effectiveness:**
- Land stewardship model reduces long-term costs
- Resident-to-steward pathway creates self-sustaining operations
- Job training integrated with housing reduces dependency
- Lower overhead than traditional shelter systems

**Holistic Approach:**
- Housing + employment + recovery support + legal advocacy
- Faith-based foundation (opt-in, never coerced)
- Community integration, not institutional isolation
- Long-term stability, not revolving-door placement

### 2. Reentry & Criminal Justice Reform

**Program:** Housing and employment for individuals returning from incarceration

**Target Population:**
- Post-incarceration (county jail or state prison)
- Probation/parole supervision
- PCR petitioners and appellate litigants
- Individuals with barriers to traditional housing

**Services Included:**
- Stable housing with accountability structure
- On-site job training (construction, land stewardship)
- Legal advocacy and record expungement support
- Peer mentorship and community reintegration
- Employer partnerships for direct placement

**Outcomes We Track:**
- Recidivism rates
- Employment retention
- Housing stability at 6, 12, 24 months
- Probation/parole compliance

**Funding Mechanisms:**
- County reentry contracts
- NJ Reentry Corporation partnerships
- Department of Corrections collaborative agreements
