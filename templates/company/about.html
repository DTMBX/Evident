Our Mission

BarberX Legal Tech was founded to level the playing field in civil rights litigation. Too often, prosecutors and police departments have access to expensive forensic tools that defendants and their attorneys cannot afford. We believe AI-powered evidence analysis should be accessible to everyone‚Äînot just those with deep pockets.

Our Story

Founded in 2024 by civil rights advocates and software engineers, BarberX emerged from a real need: processing hundreds of hours of body-worn camera footage in civil rights cases without paying $10,000+ for commercial eDiscovery platforms.

We started with OpenAI's Whisper for transcription, added speaker identification, entity extraction, and discrepancy detection. What began as a Python script for one case has grown into a comprehensive platform used in federal civil rights litigation across the country.
