Body-Worn Camera Processing

Transform BWC footage into court-ready evidence with AI-powered enhancement and analysis.

  • Whisper AI: Transcribe audio with word-level timestamps (MIT license)
  • pyannote.audio: Identify and separate speakers (officer vs civilian)
  • Real-ESRGAN: Enhance video quality with AI super-resolution (4x upscaling)
  • YOLOv8: Detect objects, people, weapons, vehicles in footage
  • FFmpeg: Stabilize shaky footage, normalize audio, extract frames

Document Intelligence

Extract, analyze, and search across police reports, discovery documents, and legal filings.

  • sentence-transformers: Semantic search finds relevant documents by meaning
  • Chain of Custody: SHA-256 checksums verify document integrity
  • Metadata Extraction: Capture creation dates, authors, edit history
Cost Savings: Tesseract is free vs $1.50 per 1,000 pages for cloud OCR. 10,000 pages = $15 saved.
