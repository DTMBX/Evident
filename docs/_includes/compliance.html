Ethical Framework

Barber Cam operates under six foundational commitments:

  • Truthful Record — Accurate, verifiable documentation
  • Non-Escalation — Recording to prevent, not provoke harm
  • Dignity — Respect for all parties involved
  • Minimum Necessary — Collect and retain minimally
  • Accountability — Published standards and transparent decisions
  • Due Process — Supporting fair outcomes, not shortcuts
