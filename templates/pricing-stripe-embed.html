Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans?

Yes! Upgrade or downgrade anytime through your account settings. Changes take effect immediately with prorated billing.

What payment methods?

We accept all major credit cards via Stripe. Enterprise customers can arrange invoice billing.

What about overages?

Founding Members (STARTER at $19/mo) have soft caps with minimal overage fees: $2/video, $1/PDF, $0.50/GB, $5/case. PROFESSIONAL has hard caps—upgrade when needed. PREMIUM and ENTERPRISE have generous soft caps.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We're HIPAA-ready with SOC 2 compliance in progress.
