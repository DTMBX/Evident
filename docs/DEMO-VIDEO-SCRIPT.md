## Recording Setup

### Screen Recording Settings

- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 (Full HD)
- **Frame Rate:** 60fps for smooth UI
- **Codec:** H.264
- **Bitrate:** 8-10 Mbps

### Audio Settings

- **Microphone:** USB condenser mic
- **Noise reduction:** On
- **Background music:** Subtle professional track (low volume)
- **Format:** 48kHz, 16-bit

### Software Recommendations

1. **OBS Studio** (Free) - Professional recording
2. **Camtasia** (Paid) - Easy editing with callouts
3. **ScreenFlow** (Mac) - All-in-one solution

## Voiceover Tips

### Tone

- **Professional** but **approachable**
- **Confident** but not arrogant
- **Energetic** but not overly excited
- **Clear enunciation**

### Pacing

- Speak slightly faster than normal conversation
- Pause briefly between scenes
- Emphasize key numbers ("10 hours â†’ 10 minutes")
- Build excitement toward the call-to-action

### Recording

- Record in quiet room
- Stand while recording (better breath support)
- Warm up voice beforehand
- Record multiple takes
- Choose best take for each segment
- Edit together smoothly
