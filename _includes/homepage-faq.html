A brief note on language

Why does Faith Frontier use “temple” language?

In Christian theology, Scripture teaches that God’s people are His temple—that He dwells within His people and forms them for holy living (1 Corinthians 3:16–17; 1 Peter 2:5).

When Faith Frontier uses “temple” language, it is a statement of religious belief and spiritual identity. It is not a claim of legal status, tax treatment, regulatory exemption, or jurisdictional authority. Any civil classification is determined separately by applicable law, filings, and competent authorities.

We affirm lawful process and public accountability; religious language does not replace statutes, courts, agencies, or due process.
