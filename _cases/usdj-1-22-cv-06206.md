# USDJ-1-22-cv-06206 – Federal Civil Rights

## Case Overview

**Docket No.:** 1:22-cv-06206  
**Court:** United States District Court, District of New Jersey (Camden Vicinage)  
**Plaintiff:** Devon Tyler Barber (pro se)  
**Defendants:** State of New Jersey; City of Atlantic City; Atlantic City Police Department; Officer John Doe 1; Officer John Doe 2

**Status:** Closed (dismissed without prejudice)

### Nature of the Action

This was a federal civil rights action brought under **42 U.S.C. § 1983**, alleging violations of constitutional rights by state and local officials. The complaint asserted claims for unlawful search and seizure, excessive force, and denial of due process. The case was dismissed without prejudice following initial pleadings and pretrial motions.
