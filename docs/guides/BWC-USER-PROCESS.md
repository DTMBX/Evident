# BWC Forensic Analyzer: User Process Guide

## 1. Upload Your BWC Video

- Go to the BWC Forensic Analyzer page.
- **Drag and drop** your BWC video file into the upload area, or click to browse
  and select a file.
- Supported formats: MP4, AVI, MOV, MKV, WMV, FLV, WebM (max 5GB).

## 2. Enter Case Metadata

- After upload, fill out the required case information:
  - **Case Number** (required)
  - **Evidence Number** (optional)
  - **Acquired By** (your name, required)
  - **Source** (e.g., OPRA Request #, required)
  - **Known Officers** (comma-separated, optional)
- Click **Start Forensic Analysis** to begin.

## 3. Wait for Analysis

- The analyzer will process your video locally (no cloud upload).
- Progress is shown with a bar and step indicator.
- Note: Analysis may take 5–30 minutes depending on video length and your
  hardware. GPU is much faster than CPU.

## 4. Review Results

- When complete, you’ll see:
  - **Duration** of video
  - **Number of speakers**
  - **Transcript segments**
  - **Discrepancies** (if any)
  - **Extracted entities**
  - **Full transcript**

## 5. Download Reports

- Download your results in multiple formats:
  - **JSON Report**
  - **Text Report**
  - **Markdown**

## 6. Start a New Analysis

- Click **Analyze Another Video** to repeat the process for a new file.

--

**All processing is 100% local for privacy and security. No data leaves your
device.**
