About BarberX Legal Technologies

Built with gratitude on the shoulders of open-source giants

Our Mission

BarberX Legal Technologies provides AI-powered forensic analysis tools for body-worn camera footage, helping attorneys in civil rights litigation, police accountability cases, and legal discovery.

We believe in leveraging cutting-edge technology while respecting both proprietary rights and open-source licenses. This page acknowledges the incredible work of developers and organizations whose tools make our platform possible.

License Compliance & Attribution

Every export from BarberX includes complete attribution for all third-party tools used in analysis. We strictly adhere to license requirements for MIT, BSD-3-Clause, Apache 2.0, and LGPL licenses.
