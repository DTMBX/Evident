        From: Mr. Barber
  To: New Jersey Department of Transportation
  Date: December 18, 2025
  OPRA REQUEST – NEW JERSEY DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION

Toll Infrastructure, Gantry Deployment, and Project Funding Records

Dear OPRA Custodian,
New Jersey Department of Transportation,

Please accept this electronic request for public records pursuant to the New Jersey Open Public Records Act (OPRA), N.J.S.A. 47:1A-1 et seq., and the common-law right of access.

Pursuant to N.J.S.A. 47:1A-6(f), this email constitutes a valid OPRA request.

This request is made:
	•	for public-interest and informational purposes only,
	•	not for any commercial use, and
	•	not to interfere with any pending legal proceeding.

STATEMENT OF PUBLIC INTEREST

NJDOT oversees transportation infrastructure affecting millions of New Jersey residents. The deployment of gantries, camera systems, and automated toll infrastructure has cost, privacy, and due-process implications warranting public transparency.

SCOPE OF REQUEST

All government records created or maintained from January 1, 2020 to present relating to:
	•	gantry installation or upgrades,
	•	camera-based toll infrastructure,
	•	coordination with NJTA,
	•	funding, planning, or approval of such projects.

RECORDS REQUESTED

I. INFRASTRUCTURE PROJECTS
	1.	Records identifying all gantry, camera, or plate-reader installations or upgrades on New Jersey toll roads, including:
	•	project names or numbers,
	•	locations,
	•	dates of installation,
	•	stated purpose (tolling, enforcement, analytics).

II. FUNDING & APPROVAL
	1.	Records reflecting:
	•	funding sources,
	•	capital approvals,
	•	federal or state grants,
	•	coordination with NJTA or other agencies.

III. CONTRACTS & PROCUREMENT
	1.	Contracts, subcontracts, or agreements administered or reviewed by NJDOT relating to:
	•	toll-related infrastructure,
	•	data or camera systems,
	•	integration with E-ZPass or toll-by-plate systems.

IV. INTER-AGENCY COMMUNICATIONS
	1.	Communications between NJDOT and:
	•	NJTA,
	•	Office of the Governor,
regarding toll infrastructure, gantry expansion, or automated enforcement systems.

NON-EXISTENCE CERTIFICATION

If requested records do not exist, please provide written confirmation stating:
	•	that no such records were created or maintained, and
	•	the reason for their absence.

FORMAT, FEES & RESPONSE
	•	Provide records electronically where available.
	•	Redact only information exempt under OPRA.
	•	Please advise in advance of any fees; I do not consent to fees exceeding $25 without approval.
	•	OPRA requires a response within seven (7) business days.

Please confirm receipt. If you are not the proper custodian, kindly forward this request and identify the correct custodian.

Respectfully submitted,
/s/ Devon Tyler Barber
